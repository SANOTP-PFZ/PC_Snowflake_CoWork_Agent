## Redesign Plan — Match Wireframe Card Style

### What the wireframe shows (reference: `sample_wireframe.png`)

Each agent card is:
- White card, rounded corners (~12px), subtle border + shadow
- **Top-left**: Small purple circular icon (agent icon)
- **Top-right** (optional): A badge/tag (e.g. "WEEKLY", "MONTHLY")
- **Title**: Bold, blue/teal colored, left-aligned (~16px)
- **Description**: Gray text, 1 line, below title
- **Bottom-left**: "Cortex Agent" chip (small purple square + text)
- Cards in a **3-column grid**
- The entire card is clickable
